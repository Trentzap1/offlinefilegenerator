Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our secure offline operations.

How does TT work without internet or mobile data?

The system communicates entirely via standard cellular network SMS protocols. It processes requests at our physical airgapped location, bypassing the need for mobile data or Wi-Fi.

How is the enterprise setup and whitelisting handled?

Our Enterprise plans include complete provisioning, device whitelisting, and setup. Contact our sales team to receive a tailored onboarding package.

How is our intellectual property protected?

Requests are handled by an isolated compute node and are permanently destroyed in memory immediately after generation. We maintain no connection history, user profiling databases, or search records.
